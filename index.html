Introduction

Adams Yang ("we", "our", or "us") operates the YourCard mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using YourCard, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When using YourCard, you may provide the following information:

  • Flashcard Content: Text, images, and study materials you create or import
  • Photos and Camera Access: Images you capture or select for flashcard creation using OCR (Optical Character Recognition)
  • Study Progress: Your quiz scores, study sessions, and learning statistics

Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use the App:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, session duration, and interaction patterns
  • Performance Data: Crash reports and diagnostic information to improve app stability

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  1. App Functionality: To provide core features including flashcard creation, studying, quizzes, and progress tracking
  2. OCR Processing: To convert images to text for flashcard creation
  3. AI-Powered Features: To generate quiz questions and study materials
  4. Notifications: To send study reminders and notifications (only if you enable this feature)
  5. App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience
  6. Technical Support: To respond to your requests and provide customer support

Data Storage

Local Storage

All your flashcard data, study progress, and personal content are stored locally on your device. We do not store your flashcard content or personal study data on our servers.

No Account Required

YourCard does not require user registration or account creation. All data remains on your device.

Third-Party Services

YourCard uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads.

Information AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information and location
  • Ad interaction data

AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Opt-out of personalized ads:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • More info: https://support.google.com/ads/answer/2662856

Google ML Kit (OCR)

We use Google ML Kit for text recognition (OCR) from images. Image processing occurs on-device, and images are not sent to external servers.

ML Kit Privacy: https://developers.google.com/ml-kit/terms

Google Generative AI (Gemini)

We use Google's Generative AI API to generate quiz questions from your flashcard content. Text data is sent to Google's servers for processing.

Google AI Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

  • Camera: To capture images for flashcard creation and OCR text extraction
  • Photo Library: To select existing photos for flashcards
  • Notifications: To send study reminders (optional, only if enabled by user)

  • Storage: To save flashcard data and images locally on your device

You can manage these permissions in your device settings at any time.
